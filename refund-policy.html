Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

  • Customers may cancel an order only before food preparation begins.
  • Once the restaurant starts preparing the order, cancellation may not be possible.

2. Advance Payment Refund

  • If KashRun or the restaurant cancels the order, the full advance amount will be refunded.
  • If the customer cancels a confirmed order, the advance payment may not be refundable.

3. Full Payment Refund

  • If a fully paid order is not delivered, the customer will receive a full refund.
  • Refund processing may take 3-7 business days.

4. Wrong or Missing Items

  • If you receive incorrect or missing items, please contact us within 1 hour of delivery.
  • After verification, a partial or full refund may be issued.

5. No Refund Cases

  • Incorrect address provided by customer.
  • Customer unavailable at delivery location.
  • Fraudulent or fake orders.
  • Claims that cannot be verified.

6. Refund Method

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method whenever possible.
  • COD order refunds may be processed through UPI or bank transfer.
