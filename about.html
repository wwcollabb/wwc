About KashRun

Kashmir's local food delivery platform bringing delicious meals directly to your doorstep.

Our Story

KashRun was created with a simple vision: to make food delivery fast, reliable and accessible across Kashmir. We aim to connect customers with their favorite local food providers and ensure a smooth ordering experience from start to finish.


While we are currently delivering in selected service areas, our goal is to expand and serve every corner of Kashmir in the near future.
