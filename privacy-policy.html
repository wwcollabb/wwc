Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order on KashRun, we may collect the following information:

  • Customer Name
  • Phone Number
  • Alternative Phone Number
  • Delivery Address
  • Pincode
  • Order Details
  • Payment Information (UTR / Transaction ID)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To contact you regarding your order.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To prevent fraudulent or fake orders.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Customer data is securely stored and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Payment Information

KashRun may collect payment-related details such as UTR numbers for payment verification purposes. We do not store any sensitive banking credentials such as ATM PINs, passwords, or UPI PINs.

5. Third-Party Services

KashRun uses trusted third-party services including Firebase Firestore and payment platforms for order processing and storage.

6. Cookies

KashRun may use browser storage technologies and cookies to enhance your experience and improve website functionality.

7. User Rights

  • You may contact us to update your information.
  • You may request correction of inaccurate information.
  • You may contact us regarding privacy concerns.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

KashRun reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice.
