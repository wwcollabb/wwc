Our Story

The Journey

2019
Idea Started

A spark of an idea — connecting creators with brands in a meaningful, fair way. What started as a vision between friends became the foundation of something real.

2020
Community Began

Our first creators joined. A WhatsApp group turned into a genuine community. We started making introductions, building trust, and seeing real collaborations happen.

2022
Collaborations Expanded

We built the platform, launched matching systems, and helped hundreds of creators land their first paid collaborations. The dream was becoming reality, one match at a time.

2024
Thousands Reached

10,000+ posts milestone. Thousands of creators registered, campaigns launched, brands matched. WWC had become a real ecosystem — and we were proud of every moment.

2026
Final Chapter

After years of building, connecting and growing — we make this difficult decision. Not because we lost hope, but because we respect what we built too much to let it fade quietly.

The Truth

Why We Decided To End WWC

Running Worldwide Collab was an unforgettable experience for us.

Over the years, we connected creators, built communities and created real opportunities for real people — many of whom had never imagined working with a brand before us.

However, maintaining and growing the platform became increasingly difficult. Limited funding, rising operational costs, and the relentless pressure of management behind the scenes took a toll that we could no longer sustain.

We put everything we had into this — our time, our energy, our passion. And we gave it everything.

After careful, honest consideration, we decided it was the right time to officially conclude this journey — with our heads held high.
